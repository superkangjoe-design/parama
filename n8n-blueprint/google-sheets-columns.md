# Google Sheets Columns

Gunakan sheet bernama `Leads`.

Kolom yang disarankan:

1. `timestamp`
2. `wa_number`
3. `customer_name`
4. `city`
5. `selected_package`
6. `last_intent`
7. `lead_score`
8. `handoff_status`
9. `last_message`
10. `ai_reply`
11. `notes`
12. `source`
13. `created_at`
14. `updated_at`

## Nilai Penting

### `lead_score`

- `cold`
- `warm`
- `hot`

### `handoff_status`

- `no`
- `qualified`
- `handed_to_admin`
- `closed`

### `source`

- `whatsapp`
- `website_widget`
- `manual`
